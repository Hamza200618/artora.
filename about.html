About Artora

Redefining Art Experiences

Founded in 2010, Artora Gallery began as a small passion project by art enthusiasts who believed in making exceptional art accessible to everyone. What started as a modest physical gallery in New York has now blossomed into a global platform connecting artists and art lovers worldwide.

Our mission is to break down barriers in the art world by providing a space where emerging and established artists can showcase their work to a diverse audience. We believe art should be experienced, not just viewed, and we strive to create meaningful connections between creators and collectors.

With our innovative virtual gallery technology and carefully curated exhibitions, we're changing how people discover, experience, and collect art in the digital age.
