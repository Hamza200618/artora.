Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid on an artwork?

To place a bid on an artwork, navigate to the auction page, select the artwork you're interested in, enter your bid amount in the bid field, and click "Place Bid". You'll need to be logged in to your Artora account to place bids.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some cases, cryptocurrency for high-value transactions.

How is shipping handled for purchased artworks?

All artworks are professionally packaged and insured during shipping. We work with specialized art shipping companies to ensure safe delivery. Shipping costs are calculated based on the artwork's size, weight, and destination.

Can I return an artwork if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 14-day return policy for most artworks. The artwork must be returned in its original condition and packaging. Some commissioned or custom pieces may not be eligible for returns. Please see our Returns Policy for complete details.

How do I become a featured artist on Artora?

We're always looking for talented artists to feature on our platform. Please visit our "For Artists" page and submit your portfolio for review. Our curation team will evaluate your work and get back to you within 2-3 weeks.

How can I verify the authenticity of an artwork?

All artworks sold on Artora come with a certificate of authenticity signed by the artist or their estate. For high-value pieces, we also provide provenance documentation and may facilitate third-party authentication services upon request.
